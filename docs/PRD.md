# Lumora — Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Overview
Lumora is an AI-powered creative studio that enables filmmakers, educators, content creators, and storytellers to generate complete cinematic productions from simple text prompts.

## Problem Statement
Creating high-quality video content requires expensive tools, large teams, and significant time. Lumora removes these barriers by combining AI image generation, video synthesis, voice cloning, script writing, and character animation into a single platform.

## Target Users
- Independent filmmakers
- YouTube content creators
- Educators and e-learning producers
- Brand designers and marketers
- Screenwriters and storytellers
